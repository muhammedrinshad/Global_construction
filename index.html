INTRODUCTION

Global Construction Contracting Co. (GCC), an ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018 certified, 100% Saudi-owned company, established in 2008 by highly experienced professionals to become a specialized Industrial contractor providing material supply and installation services for Industrial Insulation, Fireproofing and Waterproofing sectors. GCC, is the Fastest growing Waterproofing solutions provider, Emerging market leader in providing complete Industrial Insulation solutions & Emerging market leader in providing Fireproofing solutions. GCC is approved and prequalified by Saudi Aramco for the General Bid Slate in the category of Insulation & Fireproofing Services and also certified by Global Achilles for the Chemicals & Allied Industries Community. We are committed to reducing energy consumption, conserving energy, and enhancing client assets with best in class insulation and waterproofing systems. GCC is capable to execute all projects in very professional and time framed circumstances with proper quality and safety management systems, as part of our commitment to excellence. Coupled with our broad-based capabilities, it is the driving force behind our long term customer relationship and positive reputation in the industry. Strong dedication and commitment to our client forms the very fabric of our company. GCC sustains a high level of excellence, quality, and exemplary service.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of industrial insulation, fireproofing, and waterproofing services in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, recognized for our commitment to safety, quality, and innovation. ​

Our Mission

To deliver superior Industrial insulation, Fireproofing, and Waterproofing services that enhance the safety, efficiency and sustainability of our Clients' operations. Values

OUR SERVICES

Industrial Insulation

GCCC’s Insulation division evolved from a major supplier of insulation materials to an installer with an induction of a project management team with vast experience in insulation projects in various plants across the kingdom. GCCC has carried out insulation projects to piping, vessels, tanks and equipment on various prestigious projects such as Aramco, Petro-Rabigh, Sabic, Maaden Plants, Yanbu, Sadara etc.
  • THERMAL (HOT) INSULATION
  • COLD INSULATION

  • CRYOGENIC
  • HVAC INSULATION

Fireproofing

GCCC’s fireproofing services include comprehensive project planning and execution to ensure optimal fire protection while meeting specific industry requirements and safety standards. Whether it’s a refining or petrochemical plant, cementitious fireproofing is an effective way to mitigate factors that would escalate a fire and cause serious damage. GCCC provides guaranteed quality and satisfactory work.
  • PASSIVE FIREPROOFING
  • CEMENTITIOUS FIREPROOFING
  • APPROVED APPLICATORS OF AVI, TEXTRON, THERMAL SCIENCE & CARBOLINE PRODUCTS
